## Brand History
- Established: 1991
- Headquarters: Wedemark, Germany
- North American License Holder: HazeBase America

## Brand Description
Hazebase continually provides superior foggers and hazers. The brand embodies quality
and excellence in manufacturing standards and unsurpassed customer service. All machines,
accessories and fluids are analyzed under the strictest standards of quality control.

## Key Selling Points
- Designed, engineered and manufactured in Germany
- Pharmaceutical-grade, water-based fog and haze fluids
- Wide range of foggers, hazers, accessories and fluids
- Latest technology for ultimate fog and hazer performance
- Established over 30 years in the industry
