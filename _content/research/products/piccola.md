# piccola — Product Spec

**Type:** Battery-powered fog machine
**Category:** Fog Machine

## Power & Electrical
- Power Consumption: 200W at 12V DC
- Battery: Lead gel, 12V / 3.4 Ah
- Charger: 15W / 18V DC, wide-range input (90–264V)

## Performance
- Heating Time: 1 second
- Fluid Tank Capacity: 250ml
- Fog Density Settings: 25%, 50%, 75%, 100%, Single Shot mode
- Battery Life: 30 min (25%) / 10 min (50%) / 6 min (75%) / 4 min (100%)
- Fluid Consumption: 1.5ml/min (25%) / 3.0ml/min (50%) / 4.5ml/min (75%) / 6.0ml/min (100%)

## Control & Protocols
- Supported Protocols: DMX-512, RDM
- Control Options: Radio remote, cable remote (5-pin XLR)

## Physical Specifications
- Dimensions: 203 × 79 × 163 mm
- Weight: 2.6 kg

## Compatible Fluids
- base*B (250ml and 5L sizes)

## Optional Accessories
- Radio remote control
- Cable remote control (5-pin XLR)
- Hose cover
- 5-pin XLR cable whip
