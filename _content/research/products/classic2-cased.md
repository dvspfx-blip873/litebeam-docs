# classic² cased — Product Spec

**Type:** Vaporizer fog machine in flight case
**Category:** Fog Machine

## Power & Electrical
- Power: 115V~ 60Hz 1700W (North America) / 230V~ 50Hz 1600W (EU)
- Connector: PowerCON TRUE1
- Heating Time: ~12 minutes

## Performance
- Fog Output: Adjustable 1–100% in 1% increments
- Ejection Distance: Up to 15 metres
- Fog Duration: Up to 20 sec (full power) / 50 sec (full-time mode) / permanent
- Fluid Consumption: Up to 240ml/min (full power) / 40ml/min (continuous mist)
- Tank Capacity: 5 litres

## Control & Protocols
- Display: 1.3″ OLED with membrane keypad
- Interfaces: 5-pin XLR In/Out
- Supported Protocols: DMX512, RDM, Modbus RTU, JT-Remote
- Control Methods: DMX/RDM, cable remote, radio remote, integrated timer, standalone

## Physical Specifications
- Dimensions: 599 × 260 × 453 mm (flight case, L×W×H)
- Weight: 14.0 kg
- Operating Temperature: +5°C to +45°C

## Compatible Fluids
- base*X (extremely long-lasting)
- base*L (long-lasting)
- base*M (normal persistence)
- base*Q (fast-dissolving)

## Optional Accessories
- Radio remote control (Communicator)
- Cable remote control (Commander)
