# THE FAB² — Product Spec

**Type:** Hazer — The Hazer Revolution Refined
**Category:** Hazer

## Power & Electrical
- Power: 500W, 100–240V~ 50–60Hz
- Connector: PowerCON TRUE1

## Performance
- Heating Time: 5 seconds
- Haze Density: 1–100% adjustable
- Fluid Tank Capacity: 1000ml
- Pump Capacity: 5.6ml/hr (1%) to 223ml/hr (100%)
- 2 axial fans: 100m³/h each, 0–100% adjustable

## Control & Protocols
- DMX Channels: 3 (pump, fan speed, angle)
- Supported Protocols: Artnet, sACN, DMX512, RDM
- Interfaces: 10/100 Mbit Ethernet (2-port switch); 5-pin XLR In/Out
- Display: 2.8″ TFT touchscreen with auto-rotation

## Physical Specifications
- Form Factor: 19″ flight case, 2U rack
- Weight: 16.6 kg (37.5 lbs)

## Features
- 2 motorized air vanes (±45° swiveling)
- Integrated sensors: fan speed, accelerometer, temperature
- 4× NiMH batteries for cordless operation

## Compatible Fluids
- base*V
- base*B

## Optional Accessories
- Commander controller
- Communicator radio remote control
