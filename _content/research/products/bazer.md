# BAZER — Product Spec

**Type:** Hazer — Next Level Hazer
**Category:** Hazer

## Power & Electrical
- Power: 330W, 90–240V~ 50–60Hz
- Connector: PowerCON TRUE1

## Performance
- Heating Time: ~5 seconds
- Haze Density: 1–100% adjustable
- Tank Capacity: 2 litres
- Fluid Consumption: ~6.5 hours at max output / ~35 hours at lowest setting
- Fan: Centrifugal, 250m³/h, 1–100% adjustable

## Control & Protocols
- DMX Channels: 2 (fluid pump, fan speed)
- Supported Protocols: DMX512, RDM, JT Remote
- Display: 1.3″ OLED with membrane keypad

## Physical Specifications
- Dimensions: ~320 × 240 × 345 mm (in flight case)
- Weight: 11 kg

## Features
- Quick-change evaporator via service flap
- Comes standard in flight case

## Compatible Fluids
- base*B

## Included
- Flight case
